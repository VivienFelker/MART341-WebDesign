Meet the Masseuse

Melissa Robbins

Born and raised in Montgomery, Alabama. Melissa graduated from Montgomery School of Bodywork and Massage in 2007, with a concentrated study in therapeutic and orthopedic massage. She is state licensed and board certified with 15 years of experience. Melissa moved to Elk Rapids in 2007 after graduating and began her practice. Throughout the years she found herself drawn to trigger point work and the more therapeutic approach to massage therapy, along with using her intuition to guide her. Melissa’s goal is to work with each client to determine their individual needs unique to through open communication, and building a strong rapport.

When she is not working she enjoys time with her 3 children Zachary, Maxwell, and Miss Charlie. Also, working in her garden and “being” in nature with her two golden-doodles Harrison and Frankie.
